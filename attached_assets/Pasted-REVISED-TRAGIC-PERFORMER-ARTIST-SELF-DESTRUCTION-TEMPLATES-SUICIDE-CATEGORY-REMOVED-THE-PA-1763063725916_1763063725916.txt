**STRUCTURAL PATTERNS:**

**Type A - The Warning Nobody Heeds:**
Friends/family see spiral, interventions fail, person dies preventably (Belushi, Farley, Hoffman)

**Type B - The System That Destroys:**
Industry creates addiction, then abandons (Judy Garland, child stars, Elvis)

**Type C - The Persecution:**
Authority crushes artist for transgression (Lenny Bruce, Billie Holiday arrested on deathbed)

**Type D - The Repeated Pattern:**
Someone dies, next person follows exact same path despite knowing the end (Farley after Belushi)

**Type E - The Slow-Motion Suicide:**
Everyone watches destruction over years, person dies exactly as predicted (Amy Winehouse)

**Type F - The Comeback Interrupted:**
Clean/sober/recovering, then sudden death from relapse or other cause (Chet Baker, Hoffman)

**Type G - The Suspicious Death:**
Official story vs. conspiracy theories, truth unknowable (Marilyn Monroe, Natalie Wood)

**Type H - The Double Life Collapse:**
Public persona vs. secret reality, disease/death forces revelation (Rock Hudson, closeted stars)

**Type I - The Forced Outing:**
Illness cannot be hidden, media investigation, choice between coming out or lying while dying (Arthur Ashe)

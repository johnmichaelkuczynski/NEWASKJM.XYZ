USAGE NOTES
=========================================

These quotes indexed for:
1. **Fiction Writing**: Dostoevskian voice, psychological depth
2. **Quote Generator**: Direct extraction from works
3. **Model Builder**: Dostoevskian framework references
4. **Philosophical Fiction Writer**: Voice consistency checking

When generating fiction in Dostoevsky's voice:
- Deploy fevered psychological intensity
- Show inner torment visibly
- Use Biblical resonance (Lazarus, resurrection)
- Paradoxical moral hierarchy (Sonya > Raskolnikov)
- No ironic distance
- Everything at stake (salvation or damnation)
- Russian Orthodox spiritual depth
- Polyphonic (characters think independently)
